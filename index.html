Legislation upload

Instructions
  1. Navigate to congress.gov's legislation search
  2. In the sidebar, ensure that the desired congress is selected
  3. Press "Download Results", in the resulting dialog, check "Cosponsors"
  4. Press "Download Results" in the dialog to download the CSV
  5. Configure the minimum "Introduction Date" on this page
  6. Press "Choose File" and upload the downloaded file
